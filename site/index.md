# Jeraenium

Jeraenium is a living greenhouse, garden, sensor, ecology, and humane-AI project.

It combines plants, weather, ESP32 sensors, local-first data, manual observation, and AI-assisted interpretation to better understand a small living environment over time.

## What this is

Jeraenium is part garden notebook, part greenhouse observatory, part environmental sensing project, and part experiment in human-AI collaboration.

## Current focus

- greenhouse temperature, humidity, pressure, light, VOC and NOx sensing
- manual garden and plant logging
- daily condition reports
- ESP32-based monitoring
- long-term ecological pattern recognition
- useful, calm, evidence-informed AI interpretation
